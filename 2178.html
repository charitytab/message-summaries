Modern Events Are Made Clear By Prophecy

Minister: Brother William Branham
Date Preached: Monday, December 6, 1965
Location: Orange Bowl Restaurant, San Bernardino, California
Reference: 65-1206

Summary

God's unchanging method is to foreknow His people before events occur, revealed through His prophets and confirmed by the vindication of His Word in each age.

Scripture Foundation

  • Luke 17 (recorded beginning).

Also referenced: Amos 3:7, Hebrews 1:1, Hebrews 13:8, John 1:1, Matthew 11:1-11, Malachi 3:1, Malachi 4:5-6, Luke 17:28-30.

Main Points

  • The Pattern of Divine Foreknowledge. God has never failed to reveal His purposes to His servants the prophets before events occur. This unchanging method ensures that believers are never caught unaware, but must seek understanding through Scripture and prophecy.
  • The Seed Principle and Church Ages. The Church, like wheat, fell into the earth during the Dark Ages and must be buried (die) before resurrection. God has ordained a progression through seven church ages, with each reformation (Luther, Calvin, etc.) representing stages of growth, moving toward the final Bride age.
  • Prophecy Must Be Vindicated, Not Interpreted by Theology. The Bible cannot be known through education or theological study alone; it must be revealed by inspiration. Human interpretation (Methodist, Baptist, Pentecostal creeds) produces division, but God vindicates His Word through the prophet of the hour without need for outside interpretation.
  • The Eagle Illustration: Recognizing the Elect. Those truly ordained to be sons and daughters of God are "eagles"—when they see the Word of God made manifest and vindicated in power, they cannot help but run to it, regardless of their religious background. The spiritually dead (hybrids) cannot respond to the genuine revelation.
  • Spiritual Decline and Perverted Seed. Modern events reveal corruption through social perversion, moral compromise in churches (rock-and-roll in worship, fashion over holiness), and the mixing of races and bloodlines—all pointing to a hybrid, degenerate condition like unto Sodom and Gomorrah, the sign of the end-time age.

Final Exhortation

Believers must awaken to the signs of the age before it is too late. By comparing current world events to Scripture, we can see that we are living in the Bride age, not any earlier denominational era. The Holy Spirit's call is to return to the apostolic Word and its power, not to human creeds. Those who are truly born of God—the eagles—must hear the prophet's cry to leave the barnyard of denominationalism and recognize the Mother's call. The combine (rapture) is coming; prepare now by aligning with God's vindicated Word of the hour, not the stale forms of yesterday.
