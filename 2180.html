Things That Are To Be

Minister: Brother William Branham
Date Preached: Sunday, December 5, 1965
Location: Life Tabernacle, Rialto, California
Reference: 65-1205

Summary

God has promised His people a future eternal home in His Father's house, and believers must understand their predestined identity as sons and daughters of God expressing His Life to this age.

Scripture Foundation

  • Saint John 14:1-7
  • Hebrews 13:8
  • John 1:1

Isaiah 7:14; Hebrews 7

Main Points

  • The Promise of an Eternal Destination. Jesus assured His disciples that He was going to prepare a place for them in His Father's house, with many mansions, so that where He is, they may be also. This is not an ending in death but a continuation of life in another realm.
  • God's Word Is Infallible and Foreordained. Every word God speaks must come to pass; nothing happens by chance. God foreknew and pre-planned each person's existence generations before their birth, and all His promises anchored in Scripture must be fulfilled.
  • The Vindication of God's Word in Every Age. Throughout history, God vindicates His Word through signs, wonders, and the manifestation of His presence. Each age has its messenger and message—Noah, Moses, Jesus, Luther, Wesley, and Pentecost—each vindicating God's Word for their time.
  • The Nature of Believers as God's Gene and Attributes. Sons and daughters of God must possess Eternal Life, which is God's own Life expressed in human form. Before the foundation of the world, this eternal gene existed in God; believers are the manifestation of that divine attribute.
  • The Responsibility to Express God's Life. Being born again means the washing of the water of the Word has produced fellowship with God the Father. Believers are placed on earth to express God's Life to their nation and neighborhood, fulfilling their pre-planned purpose.

Quote

"Let not your hearts be troubled: if you've believed in God, believe also in me. In my Father's house is many mansions...I go and prepare a place for you."

Brother Branham opens with Christ's foundational promise of an eternal future for His people, establishing the comfort and assurance believers should have regarding life after death.

"I believe that God, knowing that we in our little finite mind could not interpret His great mind, He interprets His Own Word, by vindicating that Word in Its season."

The minister explains that God doesn't require human interpretation of Scripture; He proves His Word through manifestation and vindication in each age, demonstrating His truth through supernatural acts.

"You are expressing God's Life in you, because you are a son or a daughter of God...Wherever you are, God knew that you would be here, because you have to be one of His genes, or His attributes."

Brother Branham emphasizes the believer's role as a living expression of God's eternal attributes, purposefully placed on earth to manifest the divine nature to their generation.

Final Exhortation

Understanding that you are a pre-planned, predestined son or daughter of God with Eternal Life flowing through you should transform how you live. Your presence on earth is not accidental but purposeful—you are called to express God's Life to your nation and neighborhood. As death draws near to all of us, the comfort of knowing that Jesus has prepared a place for us and that this life continues in another realm should eliminate fear and anchor our hearts in hope. Be faithful to manifest God's character and truth in your generation, for you are one of His attributes, placed in this earth at this time to bear witness to His eternal glory.
