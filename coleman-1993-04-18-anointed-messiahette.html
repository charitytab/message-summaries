Anointed Messiahette Reapers For The Final Harvest

Minister: Brother Joseph Coleman
Date Preached: Sunday, April 18, 1993
Location: Forest Hills High School, Forest Hills, New York
Subject: Another Year of Jubilee
Inspiration: Introduce New York City to the Messiah on Earth Now, Released by Malachi 4, Through His Seven Seal, Seven Thunder Message

Summary

The Church—through the anointing of the Word released by Malachi 4—are the anointed Messiahettes called to fulfill the Great Commission as reapers in the final harvest before the rapture.

Scripture Foundation

  • Malachi 4
  • John 14:12
  • Mark 16

The revelation of Jesus Christ as the Messiah on Earth in His people; Bride Age promises

Main Points

  • The Messiah is on Earth now, manifest in His people. The End-Time Message revealed through Malachi 4 declares that Jesus Christ—the Messiah—is present and manifesting Himself in His anointed believers. The person of Jesus Christ is forming signs, wonders, and miracles through His people as He did in the beginning.
  • Believers are Messiahettes—anointed Messiahs—for this hour. The anointing of the Word creates Messiahettes. When God anoints the mechanics of believers through twenty-seven years of the Message, that anointing (the Dynamics) transforms them into Messiahettes equipped with the authority and power of Christ.
  • The anointing is the mechanics and Dynamics working together. The Word (mechanics) combined with the Dynamics of God's Spirit creates the power needed. This union produces believers who can fulfill St. John 14:12 and Mark 16—doing the works of Jesus and greater works in this final hour.
  • The Bride does not go through tribulation; only the Church does. Brother Coleman distinguishes between the true Bride—raptured before tribulation—and the general Church. The Bride must be identified and known through her position in the Word and alignment with the Messiah on Earth.
  • Reapers must go after the lost and their families in this final harvest. With understanding of their position in Christ and the anointing upon them, Messiahette reapers are called to believe all promises, take the land, and go after the lost—fulfilling the Great Commission before the rapture begins.

Quote

"The very Person of Jesus Christ, will be in you, forming signs and wonders and miracles, like He did in Malachi 4."

Coleman emphasizes the literal manifestation of Jesus Christ's person and power through His believers, restoring the same signs and wonders of the apostolic age.

"Anointed Messiahs. And the greatest Message, hallelujah, brought by Malachi 4 is, 'Messiah is on Earth now', in these young people here, in His people."

The core revelation: Christ is not merely in heaven but actively present on Earth through His anointed Body, especially the younger generation of believers standing in the Word.

"The Word, the Word, the mechanics, anointed makes the Messiahettes. When God anoints your mechanics of twenty-seven years, in this Message, then that makes you a Messiahette."

The production of Messiahettes is not automatic but requires the Word (mechanics) to be anointed by God's Dynamics, creating a union of revelation and power.

Final Exhortation

Brother Coleman calls believers to grasp their position as anointed Messiahettes in the final harvest. Having received the revelation of Malachi 4 and the anointing of the Message, they must take the promised land, believe every promise, and go after the lost and their families. The rapture is at hand; there is no time to delay. The restoration of Christ's person in the Church is manifesting signs, wonders, and miracles—equipping reapers for the last day's work before the rapture takes the Bride away.
