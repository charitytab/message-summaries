Sermon Summary

The Manifested Reality Of The Glory Of The LORD

Minister: Brother Joseph Coleman
Date Preached: Sunday, September 19, 1993 AM
Location: Local Christian Assembly, Forest Hills, New York

Summary

The Manifested Reality of the Glory of the LORD is revealed through the Voice Word of God and marks the promise of the Third Phase, calling the Bride to be filled with the Shekinah Glory and separated unto Christ through Adoption.

Scripture Foundation

1 Corinthians 3:16 (Ye are the temples of God); Malachi 4 (Evening Time light); 1 Kings 19 (Elijah and the still small voice); Mark 11:23 (Ask and it shall be given); 1 Corinthians 13:2 (Charity as the fulfillment); John 14:12 (Greater works than these); Revelation 22:17 (Come and drink of the water of life)

Main Points

  • Temple Dedication and Rededication to the Third Phase. Believers must die out to the old-world system and allow Christ to lead them to Adoption, then rededicate their temples—sanctified and consecrated—to receive the Shekinah Glory, just as Solomon's temple was filled when dedicated.
  • The Promise of the Third Pull and Spoken Word Faith. Brother Branham prophesied that the church would move into a sphere of power where the sick would be healed by a word and the blind would see. The Third Pull is now breaking forth through the Voice Word, awakening Seven Thunder faith for the Bride.
  • The Glory of His Voice Word Revealing His Glory Plans. It is the manifest Glory of God's Voice Word—not effects like wind or earthquakes—that reveals the Glory plans and attracts the Bride. This Vindicated Word brings the Manifested Reality, the evening time light promised in Malachi 4.
  • The Evening Time Outpouring and the Transformation from Son of Man to Son of God. Just as Christ changed on Pentecost from Son of Man to Son of God and filled the temple with Shekinah, so in the evening time He pours Himself out again, revealing the same nature, the same power, the same Shekinah Glory in a living people.
  • Identification with the Message and Unwavering Faith in Vindication. The Bride must identify with where the full Seven Thunders Seed Germ Message is manifested, standing without fear and never wavering from the Vindicated Word Voice that reveals the Glory of the LORD, for this faith marks the true people of God.

Quote

"I believe that the church of the living God is moving into a sphere now that's going to shake the whole world."

Brother Coleman opens with this powerful declaration from Brother Branham, emphasizing that the coming Third Pull will bring healings, resurrection of the dead, and a revival that shakes the world—a literal manifestation of supernatural power in the church.

"The sick will be healed by a word; the blind will see; the deaf will hear; the dead will be raised up; and the power of God will shake the world with the church of the living God."

This prophetic word describes the terminal signs of the Third Pull and the Spoken Word ministry about to break forth, confirming the promise that power will manifest through simple utterance of faith.

"We'll be caught up in the air, to meet Him."

Describing the culmination of the Bride's journey through dedication, consecration, and receipt of the Shekinah Glory, the church comes together as one unified body and is raptured to meet the Lord.

"I've seen His Glory in Lake Champion. I've seen His Glory at Penn Harris. I heard His Vindicated Word Voice revealing His Glory plans. And the Manifested Reality of the Glory of the Lord is here now."

Brother Coleman testifies to nineteen years of personal vindication and encounters with the Shekinah Glory, declaring that the promise is now manifest in this very meeting and season.

Final Exhortation

Do not be moved by opposition, tradition, or the voices of those who do not understand the revealed Word. Die out to worldly systems and rededicate your temples—your very being—to holiness and consecration unto the Third Phase. Seek to be filled with the Shekinah Glory of God, stand identified with the Message where the full Seven Thunders revelation is manifested, and cultivate unwavering faith in the Vindicated Word Voice. The evening time light is breaking forth now; arise and shine, for His Manifested Reality is here to transform the Bride into the image of Christ and prepare her for the coming rapture.
