Sermon Summary

And His Wife Has Made Herself Ready To Hear The Last Trump

Minister: Brother Joseph Coleman
Date Preached: Sunday, November 28, 1993 AM
Location: Local Christian Assembly, Forest Hills, New York
Subject: The Sounding Of The Trump Of God Is At Hand
Inspiration: Changing From Mortal To Immortality

Summary

The Bride of Christ is preparing herself through the transformation from mortal to immortal at the sounding of the Last Trump, which is the climactic fulfillment of the Seventh Seal and the climax of the Headstone's descent in the Shout, Voice, and Trump.

Scripture Foundation

  • 1 Corinthians 15:51–52 (the Last Trump and the mystery of immortal transformation)
  • 1 Thessalonians 4:14–17 (the resurrection and rapture of the Church)
  • Revelation 19:7–9 (the marriage of the Lamb and His Wife made ready)

Cross-references: 1 Corinthians 15:53 (corruptible putting on incorruption), Revelation 19:8 (fine linen as the righteousness of saints).

Main Points

  • The Wife Has Received True Baptism of the Holy Ghost. Before going to the Resurrection, the Wife receives the True Baptism of the Holy Ghost and holds fast to Christ's Seven Spirit-Spoken Seven Thunder Voice Word Seed, making her ready to be joined with Him.
  • The Wife Is Ready to Hear the Last Trump, Not Just Anything. She has a purpose and a calling; she is prepared specifically to hear the Trump of God, the Seventh Trumpet, not merely for any experience but for this singular event that ushers in the physical resurrection.
  • Arrayed in Fine Linen, the Righteousness of Saints. The Bride is clothed in the Wedding Garment—the Baptism of the Holy Ghost—which is the righteousness of the saints, going from Faith all the way to Charity through the Spirit-filled life.
  • The Stones Are Cut According to Specifications and Brought Together in Silence. On this Third Phase, the Great Capstone (the Holy Spirit) is setting the Stones together, and when He smears Love around them and unites them, there will be no sound—it will be a Sudden Secret Coming, fulfilling the type of the Seventh Seal in silence.
  • Adoption Precedes the Last Trump. The Church must be Adopted before the Last Trump sounds; Adoption is the proof of the Spirit's work, and the Adopted ones are those whose names are written in the Book of Life, ready to be claimed as possessions when the Lamb steps down from the Mediatorial seat.

Quote

"Let us be glad and rejoice and give honour to him for the marriage of the Lamb has come and his wife hath made herself ready."

Brother Coleman emphasizes this declaration from Revelation 19:7 as the core truth of the Third Phase, showing that the Wife is not a general body but a prepared, perfected Bride ready for the Last Trump.

"When He drops down and takes a cut Stone and begin to smear Love around it, you won't hear a sound. It'll speak for Itself."

This illustration reveals that the final gathering of the Bride happens in absolute silence and secrecy, just as the Seventh Seal is sealed in silence; the proof is the manifestation itself, not outward noise or proclamation.

"And when He begins to seal it up, the silence remain. Oh, you can't break the type."

Brother Coleman shows that the silence of the Seventh Seal cannot be broken—the Seven Thunders hold the Mystery, and the Bride's unity is established in this same silence, a reflection of God's perfect design.

Final Exhortation

Brother Coleman calls the Church to make themselves ready by accepting the Coat (the Garment of the Holy Ghost) and by manifesting the works of Christ. As the Adoption Time draws near and the lunar eclipse of November 28, 1993, marks a sign in the heavens, believers are urged to cut themselves according to the Specifications of the Word and not to play any longer. The readiness is not passivity but active faith, manifesting signs and wonders, preparing the body to be changed in a moment, in the twinkling of an eye, to meet the Lord in the air.
